Summary
---
YANG library issues
--
- YANG library defines iana-if-type as import-only and therefore YANG-Push subscription 9 can't be validated.
- YANG library misses encode-json feature in ietf-subscribed-notifications and yang-push-revision-supported feature in ietf-yang-push-revision and therefore all subscription-started notifications can't be validated.

YANG deviation issues
--
- Subscription-started and stream-filter are being deviated from ietf-subscribed-notifications and therefore all subscription-started notifications can't be validated.
- module-version and yang-library-content-id YANG nodes are being deviated from ietf-yang-push-revision and therefore all subscription-started notifications can't be validated.
- push-update, push-change-update, datastore and update-trigger YANG nodes are being deviated from ietf-yang-push and therefore all push-update and push-change-update notifications can't be validated.
- message-publisher-id YANG node is being deviated from ietf-distributed-notif and therefore all push-update and push-change-update notifications can't be validated.
